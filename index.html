About the Project

Carbon Wise: Household Waste Carbon Emission Calculator

Carbon Wise is an integrated solution developed to quantify and mitigate household carbon emissions arising from waste. The project combines a mobile application and an Arduino-based embedded system to ensure inclusivity and accessibility across diverse populations.


The mobile app enables users to input waste type and quantity, instantly calculating carbon emissions using scientifically validated factors, and offers AI-driven recommendations to promote sustainable practices. Complementing this, the Arduino system autonomously measures waste weight, categorizes it, and provides real-time emission data without the need for external devices or internet connectivity.


By making individual waste impact visible and actionable, Carbon Wise empowers households and communities to adopt responsible consumption patterns. It advances environmental education, supports climate action initiatives, and contributes directly to the Sustainable Development Goals, particularly SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 13 (Climate Action).
